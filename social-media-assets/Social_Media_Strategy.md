## 3. The Setup Mechanism & Workflow

> [!WARNING]
> Fully automating the creation of social media accounts using scripts (Python, etc.) is against the Terms of Service for Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and X. Bots will trigger immediate IP bans and CAPTCHAs.

Instead, here is the professional "Influencer/Agency" mechanism to set this up efficiently:

### Phase 1: The "Command Center" Setup (Manual Registration)
1. **Create a Master Email:** Ensure you have a dedicated email (e.g., `socials@rocktemp.in` or `rocktemp.services@gmail.com`).
2. **Register the Handles:** Dedicate 30 minutes to manually create the accounts on LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, and X using the email above and the `@RockTempHVAC` handle.
3. **Upload Brand Assets:** 
   - **Profile Picture:** Use the cropped `logo.png` we just created (the bright one with the dark navy background).
   - **Cover Photo (LinkedIn/Facebook/X):** Use a high-quality image of industrial HVAC piping or a commercial building skyline, overlaid with the text "Engineering Perfect Climate for Every Space."

### Phase 2: Ongoing Management (Automated)
Once the profiles are manually created, we can automate the *content distribution*.
1. **Choose a Management Tool:** Use a free/low-cost tool like **Buffer**, **Metricool**, or **Meta Business Suite**.
2. **Connect Accounts:** Link your newly created Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and X accounts to this single dashboard.
3. **Post Once, Publish Everywhere:** When you complete a new project (e.g., a chiller installation in a hotel), you upload the photo to the management tool once, write one caption, and it pushes to all 4 platforms automatically.
